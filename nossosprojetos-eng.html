Empregando

Helping you get a job!

Employing is an employability project of the mission agency Love Givers. Due to the COVID-19 pandemic, many Brazilians lost their jobs, further aggravating social inequalities and making it difficult for citizens to access basic rights. The Love Givers agency aims to minimize the impacts caused by the pandemic, so that people who are unemployed have the opportunity to return to the job market. We want to connect people to compatible vacancies offered by companies in Belo Horizonte and Contagem, thus we want to prepare young people and adults through meetings and lectures, facilitating their hiring.

School Reinforcement

Knowledge booster!

School reinforcement was created to solve specific learning problems that can only be corrected with the attention that this type of resource allows. The Covid-19 pandemic has aggravated the problem of school learning for many children, so we understand the importance of facilitating better school performance for our children through tutoring. Reinforcement requires preparation of educators, after all, the methodology needs to be thought-provoking, interactive and fun!
